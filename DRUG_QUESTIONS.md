## üéØ **Sample Questions for Drug Information Documents**

### **Basic Drug Information Questions**

```
- What is [drug name] used for?
- What are the active ingredients in [drug name]?
- How does [drug name] work?
- What type of medication is [drug name]?
- What is the mechanism of action of [drug name]?
- What class of drugs does [drug name] belong to?
```

### **Dosage and Administration Questions**

```
- What is the recommended dosage for [drug name]?
- How should [drug name] be taken?
- Can [drug name] be taken with food?
- What is the maximum daily dose of [drug name]?
- How often should [drug name] be taken?
- What is the duration of treatment with [drug name]?
- Are there different dosages for different age groups?
```

### **Side Effects and Safety Questions**

```
- What are the common side effects of [drug name]?
- What are the serious side effects of [drug name]?
- What should I do if I experience side effects from [drug name]?
- Are there any allergic reactions possible with [drug name]?
- What are the contraindications for [drug name]?
- Who should not take [drug name]?
- Is [drug name] safe during pregnancy?
- Can [drug name] be taken while breastfeeding?
```

### **Drug Interactions Questions**

```
- What medications should not be taken with [drug name]?
- Are there any food interactions with [drug name]?
- Can [drug name] interact with alcohol?
- What happens if [drug name] is taken with other drugs?
- Are there any supplement interactions with [drug name]?
- Should [drug name] be avoided with certain medical conditions?
```

### **Storage and Handling Questions**

```
- How should [drug name] be stored?
- What temperature should [drug name] be kept at?
- How long is [drug name] good for after opening?
- Should [drug name] be refrigerated?
- Can [drug name] be crushed or split?
- How should unused [drug name] be disposed of?
```

### **Comparative Questions**

```
- How does [drug name] compare to [other drug name]?
- What are the differences between [drug name] and [other drug name]?
- Which drug is more effective: [drug name] or [other drug name]?
- What are the cost differences between these medications?
- Which drug has fewer side effects?
```

### **Specific Medical Condition Questions**

```
- How effective is [drug name] for treating [condition]?
- How long does it take for [drug name] to work?
- What are the success rates of [drug name] for [condition]?
- Are there alternatives to [drug name] for [condition]?
- What happens if [drug name] doesn't work for [condition]?
```

### **General Comparison Questions**

```
- What drugs are mentioned in these documents?
- Which drugs are used for the same medical condition?
- What are the most common side effects across all these drugs?
- Which drugs require special storage conditions?
- What drugs have the most drug interactions?
- Which medications are contraindicated during pregnancy?
```

### **Specific Drug Questions (Replace [drug name] with actual drug names)**

```
- Tell me about [drug name]
- What should I know about [drug name]?
- Is [drug name] safe for elderly patients?
- What monitoring is required with [drug name]?
- When should I contact my doctor about [drug name]?
- What are the warning signs with [drug name]?
```
